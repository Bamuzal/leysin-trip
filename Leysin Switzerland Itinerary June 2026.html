Leysin Switzerland Itinerary

Dates: June 20-30, 2026
Base: Rte de la Mosse 2, 1854 Leysin, Switzerland
Family stop: Rte des Corbelets 43, 1854 Leysin, Switzerland

Planning Buffers

  • Rte de la Mosse 2 to Rte des Corbelets 43: about 5-10 min by car/taxi, or 25-35 min walking depending on hill direction.
  • Rte de la Mosse 2 to Leysin-Feydey station / lift area: about 10-20 min walking, or a short bus/taxi hop.
  • Rte des Corbelets 43 to Leysin-Feydey / lift area: plan 20-30 min walking uphill, or 5-10 min by taxi/local bus.
  • Leysin to Aigle by mountain train: roughly 25-30 min.
  • Leysin to Geneva Airport: plan about 2.25-2.75 hours door-to-door with train changes and address transfer buffer.

Most Important Reservations

  • Book Le Kuklos, La Fromagerie, La Ferme à Fredy, MTB rental/protection, and any Lavaux tasting.
  • For travel days, use Leysin-Feydey station as the meetup point when possible to avoid hillside backtracking.

Pricing Notes

Dining prices are CHF per person, excluding alcohol unless noted. La Fromagerie menu lists fondue around CHF 25-36 and mains around CHF 22-35. Le QG menu shows many pizzas and burgers around CHF 13.50-17 plus starters/drinks. Le Leysin lists mains around CHF 28-44. Chillon adult entry is CHF 15. Glacier 3000 adult round trip is CHF 89. Lavaux tastings commonly run CHF 25-50 per person.

Sources used: La Fromagerie, Le QG, Le Leysin, Le Kuklos, Château de Chillon, Glacier 3000, SBB Aigle-Leysin rail info, and Lavaux tasting references.
